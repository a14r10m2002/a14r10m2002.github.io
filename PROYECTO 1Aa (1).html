When my missionary will receive my package?

If your missionary is in the MTC, your package will be delivered in the next 24 hours. Upon receipt of your order, by MTC instructions, the MTC post office will not receive packages on Saturdays or Sundays. If your missionary is serving a mission in the field, the packages will be sent through FedEx to the mission offices, this delivery will take between 3 to 5 working days, the missionaries and the mission president will be responsible for getting their package to his missionary.
